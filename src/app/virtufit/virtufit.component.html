How can users try on outfits using the "Wear" view?

        Using the phone's camera, users can scan themselves and virtually try on outfits.

How can users trust that their scan is accurate and secure?

  • I provided users with privacy details prior to scanning to ensure their scans remain confidential.
  • Made more usable, by providing helpful tips on lighting, positioning, and movement.

How can users visualize outfits quickly without a full body scan?

        Users with limited time or privacy concerns can visualize outfits quickly by using the Mannequin option by choosing a mannequin that reflects them

How can users have full control over their data?

        Users can easily retake or delete their scanned results and mannequin information.

How can users find the right accessories for their outfits?

  • VirtuFit suggests complementary accessories with the "Better Together" option.
  • VirtuFit suggests combinations that go well together when users try similar items or make outfit changes.
  • Plus icons on specific body parts show users different accessories they can add.

How can users customize their outfits?

  • Customize step-by-step: Users can choose the main item, then refine their choice by type and style.
  • Filter choices: Users can narrow down their choices by color, print, and sleeve length.

How can users trust virtual fitting rooms?

  • User can explore outfit from all angles with carousel

  • VirtuFit highlights outfit details and suitability to help users feel confident

  • User can see different angles, details, reviews, and suggestions for each item on its own page.

        This feature might solve users' problems with virtual fitting rooms, such as inaccurate sizing and unrealistic images.

How can users save and organize their outfit inspirations?

  • User can save outfits to wishlist by default on click 'Save' button
  • Also user can Add outfits to specific collections created in the past or on the go

        It helps user to save and organize their favorite looks

How can users track outfit cost as they shop?

  • User can see all items in the outfit, with concise details and pricing of each item, at the bottom of the screen
  • Also they can track the total cost of outfit as they add or remove items.
